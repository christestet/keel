# Governance (pre-1.0)

- **Decision system:** Keel Decision Records (`docs/kdr/`). Binding on everyone,
  including maintainers. Reopened only per their reopening clauses, with evidence.
- **Current structure:** founding maintainer(s) arbitrate KDR acceptance.
  RFC-style discussion happens in issues; silence is not consent — KDRs are
  accepted explicitly.
- **Stated destination:** an independent foundation before 1.0, with the funding
  treaty from vision.md §2 (no std/x component without a named, paid maintainer).
- **Evidence base:** the Keel Corpus (vision.md §1) — once real Keel code exists,
  every language-change proposal must include corpus impact analysis.
